Across India

Across India is an Android App which uses OCR Service hosted at

http://35.164.84.230:8081/

Please click the image below to see it in action

Using Across India it is possible to take picture of signboards in any of the Indic scripts and get it transliterated to any other.

Download the APK from here and sideload it until we arrange to make it available from Google Playstore
